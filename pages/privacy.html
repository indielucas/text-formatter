Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 29, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Text Formatter ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you provide while using our online text formatting service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our text formatting tools.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Text Data

Important: All text processing occurs locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any text content that you input into our text formatter. Your text data remains entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers.

2.2 Local Storage

Our text formatter uses your browser's local storage to save your editing history (up to 50 steps) for undo/redo functionality. This data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

2.3 Usage Analytics

We may collect basic, non-personal analytics data such as page views, browser type, and general usage patterns to help us improve our service. This data does not include any of your text content or personal information.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain our text formatting service
  • To improve user experience and functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns for service optimization
  • To ensure the security and proper functioning of our website

4. Data Security

Since all text processing happens locally in your browser, your data security is primarily dependent on your device's security. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect our website and any non-personal data we may collect. However, since we do not store your text content, there is no risk of your text data being compromised through our systems.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services for analytics and content delivery. These services may collect non-personal information about your visit. We ensure that any third-party services we use comply with privacy standards and do not have access to your text content.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage to save your editing history for undo/redo functionality. We may also use cookies for basic analytics and to improve your experience on our website. You can clear your browser's local storage and disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our website.
