Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the word count?

Our word count follows industry standards, counting words separated by spaces. It handles multiple spaces, line breaks, and punctuation correctly for accurate results.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is calculated at 200 words per minute, which is the average reading speed for adults. Speaking time uses 150 words per minute, the typical pace for presentations.

Does this tool work offline?

Yes! All text analysis happens in your browser, so you can use this tool offline once the page loads. Your text never leaves your device.

Can I analyze text in other languages?

Yes, this tool supports Unicode text and works with all languages. However, reading times are based on English reading speeds and may vary for other languages.
